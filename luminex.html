Project Overview

Luminex is a next-generation blockchain protocol designed for high scalability and security. By integrating sharding, Layer-2 rollups, and zero-knowledge proofs (ZKPs), Luminex achieves superior transaction throughput, reduced fees, and robust decentralization.

Our Adaptive Multi-Layer (AML) architecture is crafted to support a wide range of applications, from decentralized finance (DeFi) to enterprise solutions and gaming, while keeping the design simple and effective.

Architecture

Adaptive Multi-Layer (AML) Approach

Transactions are dynamically routed to the most suitable layer based on urgency and computational requirements.

  • Layer-1 (L1): Secure, sharded blockchain for critical operations.
  • Layer-2 (L2): Off-chain solutions (zk-rollups, sidechains) for high throughput.
  • Aggregator Module: Seamless coordination between L1 and L2.
Consensus Mechanisms

Luminex employs a hybrid consensus model, primarily using Proof of Stake (PoS) to balance energy efficiency with security, while also supporting alternative models as needed.

Layer-2 Solutions
  • ZK Rollups: Off-chain transaction bundling with ZKP validation.
  • Sidechains: Customizable chains for targeted applications.
  • State Channels: Enable efficient microtransactions with periodic settlements.

Key Features

Scalability

Sharding and Layer-2 integrations enable handling of high transaction volumes seamlessly.

Security

Utilizing ZKPs and dynamic validator assignment to protect against fraud and maintain data integrity.

Decentralization

Community-driven governance through on-chain proposals and voting mechanisms.

Privacy

Confidential transactions powered by ZKPs without sacrificing transparency.

Robust Monitoring

Real-time dashboards and alerts powered by integrations with Prometheus and Grafana.
